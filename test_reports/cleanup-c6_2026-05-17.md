2. `status == pending-analysis` ⇒ no validator YAMLs under `whitebox/validators/<id>/`.
3. `status == pending-analysis` ⇒ no sheet YAMLs matching `sheets/<ID>_*.yaml`.
4. `status in (done, in-progress)` ⇒ `placeholder_doc` is null (else error: clear it).
